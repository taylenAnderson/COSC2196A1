Interest In IT

My interest in information technology started a few years after we initially got our first family computer when I was inducted into making a flash website for his fathers home business. I initially started learning action-script 2.0 and attempting to make a useful interface to advertise their services. From that point on I played with different operating systems, created servers in the home for media management and network file sharing and finally migrating to smart home systems.

My choice to study at RMIT was influenced by their offering to allow a full degree to be studied online which still allowed me to work full time. They also have a good appearance in the community at large which will provide me with better job opportunities when changing my career upon completion of my degree.

I expect that this degree will teach me how to work in the IT industry by showing me how to market myself as an IT professional, as well as teach me fundamentals in learning new programming languages. I expect that by following the course to completion I will gain the skills necessary to expand and self learn as the IT field develops and new methods of achieving things surface.
